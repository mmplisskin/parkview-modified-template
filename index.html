Wellness

Parkview's team has been dedicated to preserving your pets health for over 30 years. Our goal and policy is to treat our clients as we would treat our families, and to treat each pet as if they were our own.

Diagnostics

We work with in house and IDEXX Laboratories which allows for a very fast turn around on test results. In case of an emergency, we are able to perform most tests right away without having to wait long hours or days to get results.

Surgery

We are a full service animal hospital that provides canine and feline wellness care, pet dentistry, and veterinary surgery. We always offer individualized treatment plans to promote optimal health and prevent illnesses.

We recomend regular wellness exams for pets of all ages. This is the very best thing you can do to assure the highest quality of life for your companion. The wellness exam is an opportunity for you to discuss any questions or concerns you may have about your pets health. Topics such as nutrition, dental care, mobility issues, age related changes, and vaccinations are covered.

Get Started

With our professional dental cleanings and at-home dental care plans, you can stop the build-up of plaque and tartar on your pet’s teeth. We encourage full anesthetic dental procedures and offer a non anesthetic follow up service. These cleanings will help to prevent the formation of painful and potentially life-threatening gum diseases like gingivitis or periodontitis.
